Video/Audio Formats Description
mp4 MP4 is a digital multimedia container format most commonly used to store video and audio, but it can also be used to store other data such as subtitles and still images.
mpeg-4 MPEG-4 is a group of international standards for the compression of digital audio and visual data, multimedia systems and file storage formats.
mp3 MP3 is an acronym for MPEG Audio Layer 3. Type of File Format. It is a type of container for digital multimedia. It is a format for audio coding and storage.
ogg OGG is a free and open-source multimedia container format that's used to store and play audio and video files as well as text and metadata.
